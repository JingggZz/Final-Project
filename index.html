Welcome To my world of choices

Have you heard of Decidophobia?

"Decidophobia is the Fear of Making Decisions or fear of making the 'wrong' decision."

As someone with a bit of decidophobia, I always find it difficult to make any decision. This is a little web game which not only shows my messy brain when I must to make choices, but also a chance that you have help me to choose.

Okay so confession time! Sometimes two choices can lead you to the same outcome, but oh well what can you do about it.
